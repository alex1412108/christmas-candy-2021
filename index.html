Truffles

Truffles are best enjoyed at fridge or room temperature, allow to thaw after receipt. They can be kept in the fridge for 3 days or in the freezer for up to 28 days. All truffles contain the ingredients listed for chocolate truffles, with the addition of its own ingredients.
